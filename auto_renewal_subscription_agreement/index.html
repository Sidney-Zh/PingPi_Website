PingPi Membership Auto-Renewal Service Rules

Publication Date: 2026-05-12

Thank you for choosing the membership auto-renewal service provided by PingPi (the “Service”). The Service is provided by Rongchang District Sijin Software Development Studio (“PingPi,” “we,” or “us”) based on your membership subscription needs. Before enabling the Service, please carefully read these PingPi Membership Auto-Renewal Service Rules (the “Rules”). By checking the consent box or actually using the Service, you acknowledge that you and PingPi have reached an agreement and that you voluntarily agree to be bound by these Rules.

Key Points of the Auto-Renewal Service:

  • Subscription Period: We offer a monthly auto-renewable subscription and a yearly auto-renewable subscription. The specific plan is subject to the plan you actually select in the App.
  • Subscription Price: Subscription prices are displayed on the subscription purchase page in the App and may vary by country or region according to your App Store storefront. Currently, the base prices are US$19.99 per month and US$199.99 per year, unless otherwise shown on the subscription page. Any introductory offer, if available, such as a first-month price of US$9.99 or a first-year price of US$149.99, will be displayed before purchase.
  • Billing Method: After activation, your membership will automatically renew and your membership period will be automatically extended within the plan period you selected.
  • Turning Off Renewal: You have the right to terminate the Service at any time. To cancel, you must do so at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period; otherwise, you may be charged for the next billing period.
  • Cancellation Path:

    Open the Settings app on your device → tap your Apple Account name → Subscriptions → select PingPi membership → Cancel Subscription. You may also manage subscriptions through your Apple Account settings.

  • Once you choose to enable auto-renewal for PingPi membership, you formally authorize PingPi to request the payment channel, within 24 hours before the expiration of your membership period and after expiration, to directly deduct the fee for the next billing period from your own account through Apple’s subscription system. You agree that this deduction process does not require you to verify your membership password, payment password, or SMS verification code again. The actual timing of the deduction will be subject to the specific rules of Apple App Store or the relevant payment channel.

I. Activation and Authorization

1. The Service is an ancillary feature of PingPi membership services, intended to provide you with an uninterrupted membership experience and avoid interruption of benefits due to forgetting to renew.

2. Applying to activate the Service means that you irrevocably authorize PingPi, through Apple Inc., to deduct the subscription fee for the next billing period from your Apple Account when your membership is about to expire, usually within 24 hours before expiration.

3. You are responsible for ensuring that your Apple Account has a valid payment method or sufficient available balance. If deduction fails due to insufficient balance or account abnormalities, PingPi has the right to suspend or terminate your membership service, and any resulting interruption of benefits shall be borne by you.

II. Billing and Renewal Rules

1. Automatic Extension

Once billing is successful, your membership validity period will be automatically extended by one billing period, monthly or yearly, on top of the existing term. The Apple App Store subscription record will serve as proof of successful billing.

2. Price Adjustment Mechanism

PingPi has the right to adjust membership subscription prices according to business development. If the price changes when your subscription is renewed automatically, the then-current effective price will apply, or your consent will be obtained before billing, as specifically governed by the relevant policies of Apple App Store.

3. Billing Timing

According to Apple’s subscription rules, the billing instruction is usually initiated within 24 hours before the end of the current period. Please pay attention to the status of your account funds and payment method.

III. Cancellation and Service Termination

1. Voluntary Cancellation

You may choose to enable or disable the Service at your own discretion. If you decide not to continue using the membership service, please cancel the subscription through the cancellation path described above in Apple Account settings at least 24 hours before the current subscription period expires.

2. Deemed Consent to Renewal

If you do not cancel your subscription within the required time, namely 24 hours before expiration, you will be deemed to have agreed to continue using the Service, and Apple’s billing instruction will remain valid. Except as required by applicable law or Apple’s applicable refund policy, once the fee for the next billing period has been successfully charged, that fee is generally non-refundable, and your membership service will continue until the end of the next billing period.

3. Validity of the Service

The Service remains valid until you voluntarily cancel it or your membership is terminated. Any billing instruction that you have entrusted PingPi, through Apple, to make before you choose to terminate the Service remains valid. Except as required by applicable law or Apple’s applicable refund policy, PingPi will not refund fees deducted based on such instruction.
